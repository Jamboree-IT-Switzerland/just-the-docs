# Install Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is the main communication platform for all Swiss Contingent coordination. Install it on your desktop and mobile device.

---

## Desktop App

Download and install the Teams desktop app for Windows or macOS:

[Download Microsoft Teams (Desktop)](https://teams.microsoft.com/download){: .btn target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}

Sign in with your Jamboree account:
- **UL / US:** `firstname.lastname@unit.jamboree.ch`
- **CMT / IST:** `firstname.lastname@jamboree.ch`

### Already installed

Click on your profile picture in the top right corner and select "Add Account".

---

## Mobile App

Search for **Microsoft Teams** in your app store:

[iOS – App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/microsoft-teams/id1113153706){: .btn target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}
[Android – Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.microsoft.teams){: .btn target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}

Sign in with your Jamboree account as above.

---

## First Steps

1. Find your **unit's channel** in the left sidebar
2. Check the **Announcements** channel for updates from the Contingent
3. Use **Chat** for direct messages to team members

---

If you have issues signing in, please contact Jamboree IT.
