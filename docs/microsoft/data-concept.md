# Data Concept & Privacy

In this doc we inform you about our current Data Concept and the Privacy Policy which applies.

## Principles

The following principles apply:
- Do not share login credentials
- No local copies of Jamboree files (especially those containing particularly sensitive data)
- Do not share particularly sensitive data

## Data Storage

All data belonging to a unit must be stored in the respective **Unit Teams**. Access is restricted to Unit Support (US) and the corresponding leaders only.

**Teams Unit Exchange** is the platform for communication and exchange between troops. However, **no documents should be stored there**.

| Platform | Purpose | Store Documents |
|---|---|---|
| Unit Teams | Unit-internal collaboration | Yes |
| Teams Unit Exchange | Cross-troop communication | No |

## Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy of Pfadibewegung Schweiz is in effect.
