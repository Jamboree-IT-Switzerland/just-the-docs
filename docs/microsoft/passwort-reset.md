# Reset Your Password

If you have forgotten your password or are locked out of your account, you can reset it via the Microsoft self-service portal.

---

## Self-Service Reset

[Reset Password (Microsoft)](https://aka.ms/sspr){: .btn target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}

1. Go to the link above
2. Enter your Jamboree account address (`firstname.lastname@unit.jamboree.ch` or `firstname.lastname@jamboree.ch`)
3. Verify your identity via the Microsoft Authenticator app or your registered backup method
4. Set a new password

Your new password must meet the following requirements:

- Minimum **12 characters**
- At least **1 uppercase letter**
- At least **1 lowercase letter**
- At least **1 number**
- At least **1 special character**

---

## No Access to MFA?

If you no longer have access to your authenticator app and cannot complete the self-service reset, contact Jamboree IT directly:

`it[ät]jamboree.ch`

Please include your full name, Pfadi name, and account address in your message.
